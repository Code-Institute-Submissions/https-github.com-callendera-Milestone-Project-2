Mission Statement

Our mission is to work towards a more accepting environment for all people, regardless of sexual orientation or gender identity through education, support, social action, and advocacy.


About

Our work is grounded in social and economical equality for people of all walks of life. The LGBTQ+ community is increasingly marginalized and stiffled, and we are here to advocate for our community.
